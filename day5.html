3D Printig


3D printing is a process of making three dimensional solid object from a digital file. It is also known as adhetive manufactring.
When we use 3D Printer?

  • custoize product
  • when products are not possible to create by traditional manufacturing
  • when production volume is low
  • when parts are obsolete and not available in the market

Application of 3D Printer


The Application of 3D Printer are:
  • Product realisation
  • Functional testing
  • Manufacturing tools (jig and fictuers)
  • Medical applications (e.g. surgical planning)
  • DIrect digital manufacturing
